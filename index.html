LYRIC QUIZ

Welcome to the Lyric Quiz

This is a quiz devised to test your knowledge of lyrics from a range of famous songs spanning a variety of genres!

For each question, you will be shown a certain lyric from a certain song, and your task is to identify which song it came from. You will be shown 4 answers, containing a song title and an artist.

Choose carefully! Once you have picked your answer, you cannot change it, unless you retake the quiz of course!

Good luck and enjoy the quiz!
